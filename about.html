About SalaryPro Intelligence

SalaryPro Intelligence is an Indian financial awareness platform designed to help salaried employees understand:

  • Take-home salary calculations
  • CTC vs In-hand salary
  • Tax regime comparison
  • Provident Fund calculations
  • Emergency fund planning
  • Financial roadmap creation

Our mission is to simplify salary planning and personal finance for Indian professionals during FY 2026-27 and beyond.

This platform is built for educational and financial awareness purposes.
