About SalaryPro Intelligence

SalaryPro Intelligence is an India-focused salary planning and financial awareness platform designed for salaried employees.

Our mission is to help professionals understand:

  • Take-home salary calculations
  • CTC vs in-hand salary
  • Income tax planning
  • Provident Fund calculations
  • Emergency fund planning
  • Financial awareness for FY 2026-27

This platform provides educational calculators and financial estimation tools to simplify salary and tax understanding for Indian employees.

We aim to continuously build useful salary and financial planning tools for users across India.
